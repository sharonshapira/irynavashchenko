- NOW TEACHING ONLINE! -


World-renowened contortionist Iryna Vashchenko teaches the art of contortion online and in person.

Iryna travels between Germany and the UK and trains students in Berlin and Leeds. She gives online lessons via Zoom or Whatsapp (For rates please see our prices page).

The lessons are suitable for all levels and ages. Irynas approach is highly individual and training techniques vary between each person according to their specific level and needs. Each training program is tailored especially for the student, focusing on achieving results with each lesson.
